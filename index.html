Lunyra Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lunyra handles information when you use the app. Lunyra is designed to support relaxation, focus, meditation, breathing, and sleep.

Information We Collect

Lunyra does not collect, store, or share personal data from users.

We do not require you to create an account, sign in, or submit personal information to use the app.

We do not collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, messages, photos, or payment details through the app.

How We Use Information

Because Lunyra does not collect personal data, we do not use your personal information for analytics, advertising, profiling, or marketing.

Any settings or preferences you choose within the app are intended only to support the app’s features on your device.

Third-Party Services

Lunyra does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services to collect personal data from users.

If this changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those services are used.

Data Retention

Since Lunyra does not collect or store personal data, we do not retain personal information on our servers.

Your Choices

You can stop using Lunyra at any time by uninstalling the app from your device.

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no user account data for you to access, correct, or delete.

Children’s Privacy

Lunyra is a general audience app and is not directed to children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
